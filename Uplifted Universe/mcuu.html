Blind Automatically fail any check related to sight
Have disadvantage and a -2 penalty on all attack rolls
Give advantage on all attack rolls against you

(4 damage on the 2nd turn, 6 damage on the 3rd)
Have disadvantage on all attack rolls
An Action required to put yourself out
Charmed Cannot willingly attack the creature that charmed you
Automatically fail any Intuition saving throw from the creature that charmed you
Chilled Have your speed reduced by one step
Cannot take reactions
If your speed is reduced below Very Slow through being Chilled, you become Frozen
Deafened Automatically fail any check related to hearing
Distracted Gain disadvantage and a 2 skill step penalty on all checks related to sensing anything not related to the distraction
Cannot use your movement or actions, including bonus actions and reactions, for anything unrelated to the distraction
Drained At 1st level, a Drained creature has a 2 skill step penalty on all Skill Checks
At 2nd level, a Drained creature has its speed reduced by 1 step, to a minimum of Very Slow
At 3rd level, a Drained creature has a 2 dice step penalty on all attack rolls, loses the bonus saving throw dice and takes an additional 1 skill step penalty on all skill checks
At 4th level, a Drained creature has its speed reduced a further 1 step, to a minimum of Very Slow
At 5th level, a Drained creature loses all BP and can no longer gain it back in any way
At 6th level, a Drained creature falls Unconscious
At 7th level, a Drained creature dies

Automatically fails Reflex and Intuition saving throws.

Has disadvantage on all attack rolls and saving throws.
Prone Has disadvantage on all melee attack rolls.
Gives melee attack rolls against the creature advantage.
Restrained Has its speed reduced to 0.
Cannot take reactions.
Has disadvantage on attack rolls and Reflex saving throws.
Stunned Has its maximum and current RPI reduced by 1 this Initiative. If the creature's maximum RPI is reduced to 0 by this effect, the creature becomes Unconscious.
Unconscious Has its speed reduced to 0.
Cannot take any actions or reactions.
Gives any attacker advantage.
Has any attack against it count as a Coup de Grace.
Automatically fails all Reflex and Intuition saving throws.
